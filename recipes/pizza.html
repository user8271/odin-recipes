Pizza

This is a simple pizza dough recipe. It can be used for all kinds of pizza and it is very simple to make.

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit and grese a pizza pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix yeast and sugar with warm water for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt. Beat mixture until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rest for 5 minutes.
  5. On a floured surface, spread dough into a 12 inch circle.
  6. Put the dough in the pizza pan and add toppings of choice.
  7. Leave pizza in the oven until it becomes golden brown.
  8. Let the pizza cool for 5 minutes, and then it is ready to serve.
