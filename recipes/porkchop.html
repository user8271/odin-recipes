Pork Chop

This is a simple pork chop recipe. It is suitable for lunch.

Ingredients:

  • Pork chops
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Olive oil
  • Unsalted butter
  • Garlic cloves
  • Honey
  • Rice vinegar

Steps:

  1. Put oven rack 6 inches from the heat source. Preheat the broiler of the oven.
  2. Season pork chops with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Heat oil on medium high until hot. Sear pork chops until golden brown and move them to a plate.
  4. Reduce heat and add butter. Add garlic, water, honey, and vinegar. Increase heat again until sauce is thickened. Baste porkchop with the sauce.
  5. Transfer it to the broiler and cook until its edges are slightly charred.
